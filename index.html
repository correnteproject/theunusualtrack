An unusual track for skiers


A short journey over time and perspective

 
 

Skiing is a sport widely practiced in the Alps, and in this regard, the italian province of Alto Adige is not the exception. Only in this province, more than 1.000 slopes are scattered over the mountains so that downhill ski, which is the most popular skiing discipline, may be practiced by both locals and tourists. Skiing then renders a widely known feature of the landscape of Alto Adige.

Within this scenario it is not difficult to track the slopes where to slide down and have fun in Alto Adige. Therefore, this website does not track slope locations as usual. Instead, it leads you to track an unnoticed aspect that you may come across while going down.

As every single unnoticed thing on earth, some perspective is always needed to appreciate it. Time, in many cases, helps us with that purpose. Then, the following track starts some years ago before it leads you to discover and track what may be hidden beneath an usual ski slope. Hold on tight and scroll down!
