What is this for?


This website is an academic project that aims at rising awarness on the use of technical snow in the province of Alto adige, Italy. Nowadays, it is not a secret that around 90% of the skislopes where to practice downhill ski around Bolzano rely on technical snow. To do so, a huge quantity of water is needed, yet it is very difficult to frame that quantity and what its allocation/consumption means in both a global scenario of water scarcity and a local scenario of water surplus.

For this reason, this project intends to portray such quantity of water in very common objects of our everyday life so that we may have a glimpse of what we are using to have a longer winter season in times of climate change.
